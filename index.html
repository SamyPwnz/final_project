Ford Mustang

The Ford Mustang is a American car manufactured by Ford. The Mustang was introduced on April 17, 1964, the 1965 Ford Mustang was Ford's most successful launch since the Model A.The Mustang created the pony car class in American cars, affordable sporty coupes with long hoods and short rear decks. It gave rise to competitors like the Chevorlet Camaro, Pontiac Firebird, AMC Javelin, and first generation Dodge Challenger.

When the Mustang came out in 1964 it costed $2,368.But the Mustang used parts from other Ford models at the time. Most of the interior, chassis, suspension, drivetrain components were from the Ford Faclon, and Fairline.
